Introduction to the Solar System

The solar system is a collection of planets, moons, and other celestial bodies that orbit around the Sun.Our Solar System contains the Sun, 8 planets, and lots of smaller objects. It formed 4,500 million years ago. It is on an outer spiral arm of the Milky Way galaxy.
