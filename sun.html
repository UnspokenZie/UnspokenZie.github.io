What is the Sun?

The Sun is a star at the center of our solar system, providing light and heat that makes life possible on Earth.
The sun is an ordinary star, one of about 100 billion in our galaxy, the Milky Way.

Fun Fact

Did you know? The Sun accounts for 99.86% of the total mass in the solar system!
